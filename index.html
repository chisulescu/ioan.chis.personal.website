Ioan Chiș

Engineering & Project Manager | Building Scalable, Impactful Systems | AI Enthusiast

My journey didn't begin in a server room, but in the heart of the Carpathian Mountains. Born and raised surrounded by nature, my childhood summers were spent in Maramureș, along the Iza Valley. There, amidst the rhythm of making hay and the shared warmth of meals cooked outdoors in a traditional cauldron (ceaun), I learned my earliest lessons about resilience, hard work, and the intricate balance of natural ecosystems.

Those formative years instilled in me a deep appreciation for systems that endure. Today, I channel that same dedication into the digital world. My fascination with technology is rooted in those same principles: building scalable, resilient structures that serve a meaningful purpose and stand the test of time.

As an Engineering Manager and AI enthusiast, I bridge the gap between strategic vision and technical execution. My professional narrative spans from orchestrating robust cloud infrastructures and guiding cross-functional teams in the FinTech sector, to nurturing the next generation of minds teaching Intelligent Systems at the Technical University of Cluj-Napoca. Most recently, I drive strategic tech initiatives and R&D for global enterprises.

I believe that whether you are cultivating a field or architecting a distributed enterprise platform, the core requirement remains the same: a profound respect for the process, the people, and the value delivered. I bring this blend of authentic grounding and cutting-edge technical leadership to every organization I partner with.

Things I Found Inspiring

Books, articles, and talks that I truly enjoyed and took inspiration from.

Asia's Cauldron

Robert D. Kaplan

A fascinating dive into the geopolitics of the South China Sea. Understanding macro-level system dynamics and geographic constraints in geopolitics often mirrors the complexity of architectural bottlenecks in distributed tech systems.

Authentic Leadership

Essential Reading

A powerful reminder that there is no single mold for a successful leader. It highlights how vastly different leaders can be and still succeed. The core message is that we shouldn't try to copy anyone else's style; instead, we must discover our own unique, authentic path to success.

The Art of War

Sun Tzu

A timeless lesson that remains profoundly relevant today. In leadership and management, as in life, the greatest victory is that which requires no battle. The most effective way to win a dispute or overcome a challenge is to prevent it entirely or resolve it long before it escalates.

The Wine Book

Matthew Jukes

An excellent, accessible guide for the world of wine. It explores what's in the bottle, grape varieties, and why we gravitate towards certain tastes. It is amazing how wine industry is reinvent itself by innovation driven by experiments -- a truly motto for all of us.

Magellan

Stefan Zweig

I was amazed to learn that the first person to circumnavigate the globe was not Magellan, but Enrique of Malacca, Magellan's personal interpreter. A fascinating story of exploration and the hidden figures behind massive historical achievements.

Axial: Business Models

Substack Article

A comprehensive breakdown of different business models and value capture mechanisms. It provides an excellent mental framework for understanding how modern companies structure themselves to build moats and scale effectively.

Axial: New Frontiers #3

Substack Article

An insightful exploration of emerging technological frontiers and deep tech investments. It offers valuable perspectives on where the next wave of fundamental innovation and scientific breakthroughs is heading.
